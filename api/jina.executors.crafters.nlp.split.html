Bases: jina.executors.crafters.BaseSegmenter

Sentencizer split the text on the doc-level into sentences on the chunk-level with a rule-base strategy.

The text is split by the punctuation characters listed in punct_chars. The sentences that are shorter than the min_sent_len or longer than the max_sent_len after stripping will be discarded.

  • min_sent_len (int) ‚Äì the minimal number of characters (including white spaces) of the sentence, by default 1.

  • max_sent_len (int) ‚Äì the maximal number of characters (including white spaces) of the sentence, by default 1e5.

  • punct_chars (Optional[str]) ‚Äì the punctuation characters to split on.

class jina.executors.crafters.nlp.split.SlidingWindowSegmenter(window_size=300, step_size=150, min_substring_len=1, *args, **kwargs)[source]¬∂

Bases: jina.executors.crafters.BaseSegmenter

SlidingWindowSegmenter split the text on the doc-level into overlapping substrings on the chunk-level.

The text is split into substrings of length window_size if possible. The degree of overlapping can be configured through the step_size parameter. The substrings that are shorter than the min_substring_len will be discarded.
